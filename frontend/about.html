About MediAssist AI

An AI-powered clinical decision support system that helps analyze patient symptoms and lab reports to assist in preliminary medical assessment — built with machine learning, OCR, and large language models.

What MediAssist AI Does

S

Symptom Analysis

Patient enters age, gender, and symptoms. A trained Random Forest / XGBoost model predicts the most likely disease with a severity score.

R

Report Analysis

Upload a CBC blood test PDF or image. OCR extracts values, the ML model flags abnormalities, and Claude AI interprets the findings.

M

Medicine Advice

Based on predicted disease, MediAssist recommends medicines, dietary advice, precautions, and workout guidance from curated datasets.

A

AI Reasoning

Claude Sonnet provides deeper clinical reasoning — differential diagnoses, test recommendations, and red flag identification.

Important Medical Disclaimer

MediAssist AI is a decision support tool only. It is not a licensed medical device, and its outputs are not medical diagnoses. The predictions and recommendations provided are generated by machine learning models trained on publicly available datasets and may not be accurate for all individuals. Always consult a qualified doctor, physician, or healthcare professional before taking any medication or making any medical decision.

For educational and research purposes. MediAssist AI does not store any patient data. All analysis is performed locally on your device and server. No personal health information is retained.
